About Me

She Showed Up Before She Was Ready

"Readiness is built through action."

Annette didn't fully understand artificial intelligence — not yet. She only knew it was coming. She sensed it the way you sense a storm before the first drop of rain hits the ground.

And instead of waiting for certainty, she chose courage.

She started an AI Club in her community — gathering curious minds, printing flyers, arranging chairs, and asking questions she didn't yet know how to answer. Week after week, people showed up because she did. She built a space for learning before she had mastered the subject herself.

Then someone said two words that shifted her trajectory: "Try Pursuit."

Pursuit.org wasn't just a program — it was a mirror. It reflected back the potential she had carried all along but had never fully named. Fellows who looked like her. Instructors who challenged her. A community that declared: You belong here. Tech needs you.

Today, Annette V. Blue is an AI-Native Builder and a Pursuit Fellow — not because she waited until she felt ready, but because she understood something deeper: readiness is built through action.

The club she created out of curiosity didn't just teach others. It transformed her.
